毕业季开学第一周总结

02/19/2016

虽然现在已经大四最后一个学期了，感觉并没有闲下来。回想上学期，虽说去年11月份前确实过得很充实，暑假都木能回家， 每天都在跑食堂二楼自习室刷G，除了10月份的时候被强制要求去外地领个证书。 而11月份的时候都是在考G和两次的考T中度过的,12月份的时候赶申请材料和deadlineo(╯□╰)o。 但是12月份中旬之后，明明没有那么多事了，却是感觉整个人都松懈了下来，在学校里剩下的日子里面实在是过得太颓废了。 一月中旬回家后，带了本编程书回家，结果都静不下心学习，来校前的两周去codecademy浏览了一下html、css和bootstrap的基础， 唉，整个假期啥正经事都没干，就利用git和github整了个静态网页:-(，这里赞一下github。BTW，本来想注册个人域名来搭， 但是由于要花钱来供养它，就罢了，等我闲得无聊 && 有收入时，再学一下node.js和php来重新搭吧o(╯□╰)o

又扯远了，2月13号就来学校了，一天半的车程也让自己想了很多，自己至少得为自己负责了，怎能随便地浪费时间呢！！！ 这周过得比颓废的时候好多了，但是自己效率还是处于悬崖底的感觉：
  • 这周“重温”（不如说是重新开始学习）了一下C++,但是效率有待提升，没有做到看与写的同步；
  • Sedgewick的算法课也没有安排好时间上，算法书一本都没开始看:-(，下周得分配好时间；
  • 毕业设计终于开始了，不过看过的论文还是处于一知半解的程度，整体的模块划分也没开始。 感觉应该每次尽量地针对看过的论文,做做笔记，记录一下其中的不懂的，整理在note部分。 周末应该开始分析整个系统架构的需求和相应用到的方法和类，动手写一下简单版本的基本部分的C++代码了

Application Season

02/05/2016

I do not know what happed to my application process. Every day, I become more and more upset. I used to believe that although I was not the best, I deserved a decent school. As day and day passed by, I still got nothing from any graduate school. I began to get annoyed easily and doubt myself. Nevertheless, considering the fact that I do not have any paper published in Top Conference or any internship, it is hard for me to stand out in a big pool of qualified pools. I am not strong enought to attract my dream schools. Besides, it is a really bad strategy for not contacting POIs. Another stupid mistake I made is that I only applied to only a few number of schools, which are hard to get admitted from admission data published in past few years,without giving a thought on other schools. I feel sad for the past three month I have wasted in doing nothing.It's time for me to make a change and prepare for the worst situation -- The chance is that I might be rejected by all of those schools.

Whatever, I should stop being worried and wandering every day. From now on, I have to try to do the following things:

  1. Studying C++ as a begainner;
  2. Acquring the basic knowledge of algorithms (and data structure, if necessary);
  3. Sleeping before 00:30 while getting up at around 8:00 in the morning;
  4. Starting to focus on finishing my graduate thesis;
  5. Blogging what I have learned or what I have done in my personal website every day, if possible.
Anyway, I should move on rather than waste my time in those unnecessary self-doubts and worries. Fighting, Cuiting. You should learn to challenge your self.
