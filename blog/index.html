Application Season

02/05/2016

I do not know what happed to my application process. Every day, I become more and more upset. I used to believe that although I was not the best, I deserved a decent school. As day and day passed by, I still got nothing from any graduate school. I began to get annoyed easily and doubt myself. I do not have a paper published inn Top Conference or any internship. It was all of my fault. I got accustomed to pursuing those trival things and became scared of roads that were full of risks. I feel sad for the past three month I have wasted in doing nothing.

Probably the most urgent action I should take is to prepare for the worst -- Maybe I do not have sufficient money to support my graduate school, or I would be rejected by all the schools I have applied. I should stop being worried and wandering every day. From now on, I have to promise that:

  • I would engaged in studying C++ and recovering the basic knowledge, such as algorithm and data structure.
  • I would sleep before 00:30 and get up at around 8:00 in the morning.
  • I would spend some time in my thesis.
  • I should reflect myself in this blog every day and record the progress I make every day.
    • Anyway, I should keep going rather than wasted my time in those unnecessary self-doubts and worries. Fighting, Cuiting. You should learn to challenge your self.
